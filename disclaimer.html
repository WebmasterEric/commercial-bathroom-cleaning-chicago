This website provides a dispatch request and coordination system for commercial bathroom and restroom cleaning in Chicago.

Role & scope

Commercial Bathroom Cleaning operates as a coordinator and dispatcher. We facilitate scheduling and communication with independent cleaning crews. We do not guarantee availability, exact arrival times, or outcomes until a request is reviewed and confirmed.

Scheduling windows

Requested dates and times are request windows only. All jobs are subject to confirmation by text or phone. Emergency or same-day requests depend on availability and access constraints.

Pricing & deposits

Pricing ranges shown on this site are examples. Final pricing depends on scope, restroom count, severity, access rules, and timing. Deposits are required to begin scheduling and become non-refundable once sourcing or coordination begins.

Commercial use only

This service is intended for commercial properties only. Residential requests are not accepted through this system.

If you have questions before submitting a request, use the contact information provided on the dispatch page.
